Causes Effects Solutions

Causes of Climate Change

  • Greenhouse gas emissions from burning fossil fuels
  • Deforestation and land use changes
  • Industrial and agricultural activities

Effects of Climate Change

  • Rising global temperatures and sea levels
  • Extreme weather events like hurricanes and droughts
  • Loss of biodiversity and habitat destruction

What Can We Do?

  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle
  • Use renewable energy sources
  • Support climate policies and organizations
  • Raise awareness and educate others
